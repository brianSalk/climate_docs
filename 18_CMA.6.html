Decision 18/CMA.6
Technology implementation programme
The Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Paris
Agreement
1. Decides to launch a process for elaborating the technology implementation
programme;
2. Requests the Subsidiary Body for Implementation to consider this matter at its
sixty-second session (June 2025) based on the results of Parties deliberations thereon at this
session, as contained in the draft text available on the UNFCCC website,
1
while recognizing
that the draft text does not represent consensus among Parties, with a view to recommending
a draft decision for consideration and adoption by the Conference of the Parties serving as
the meeting of the Parties to the Paris Agreement at its seventh session (November 2025)
with a view to informing and further elaborating the technology implementation programme.
