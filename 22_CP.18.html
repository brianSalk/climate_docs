Decision 22/CP.18
Activities implemented jointly under the pilot phase
The Conference of the Parties,
Recalling decisions 5/CP.1, 10/CP.3, 13/CP.5, 8/CP.7, 14/CP.8, 10/CP.10, 6/CP.12,
7/CP.14 and 8/CP.16,
Having considered the conclusions of the Subsidiary Body for Scientific and
Technological Advice at its thirty-seventh session,
Considering that reports on activities implemented jointly have provided a rich
opportunity for learning-by-doing and that no further reports have been submitted by
Parties since 2006,
Recognizing that other channels for providing similar information exist,
Decides to conclude the pilot phase for activities implemented jointly.
