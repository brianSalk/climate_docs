Decision 5/CP.2
Linkage between the Ad Hoc Group on Article 13
and the Ad Hoc Group on the Berlin Mandate
The Conference of the Parties,
Recalling Article 13 of the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change
and the work being done by the Ad Hoc Group on Article 13,
Recalling also the work of the Ad Hoc Group on the Berlin Mandate,
Decides that the Ad Hoc Group on the Berlin Mandate may, in any consideration of a
multilateral consultative process, seek such advice as may be deemed necessary from the
Ad Hoc Group on Article 13 on this matter.
8th plenary meeting
19 July 1996
