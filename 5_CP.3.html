Decision 5/CP.3
Date and venue of the fourth session of the Conference of the Parties
The Conference of the Parties,
Recalling Article 7.4 of the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate
Change,
Recalling General Assembly resolution 40/243 of 18 December 1985,
Having received an offer from the Government of Argentina to host the fourth session
of the Conference of the Parties in Buenos Aires and to cover the related costs involved,
1. Accepts with gratitude the generous offer of the Government of Argentina to
host the fourth session of the Conference of the Parties;
2. Decides that the fourth session of the Conference of the Parties shall be held in
Buenos Aires, Argentina, from 2 to 13 November 1998;
3. Requests the Executive Secretary to conclude a host country agreement with
the Government of Argentina on arrangements for the fourth session of the Conference of the
Parties.
5th plenary meeting
5 December 1997
