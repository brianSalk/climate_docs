Resolution 1/CMP.3
Expression of gratitude to the Government of the Republic of Indonesia
and the people of the province of Bali
The Conference of the Parties and the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the
Parties to the Kyoto Protocol,
Having met in Bali from 3 to 15 December 2007 at the invitation of the Government of the
Republic of Indonesia,
1. Express their profound gratitude to the Government of the Republic of Indonesia for
having made it possible for the thirteenth session of the Conference of the Parties and the third session of
the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Kyoto Protocol to be held in
Bali;
2. Request the Government of the Republic of Indonesia to convey to the people of Bali the
gratitude of the Conference of the Parties and the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the
Parties to the Kyoto Protocol for the hospitality and warmth extended to the participants.
