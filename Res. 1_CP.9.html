II. RESOLUTIONS ADOPTED BY THE CONFERENCE OF THE PARTIES
Resolution 1/CP.9
Expression of gratitude to the Government of the Italian Republic
and people of the city of Milan
The Conference of the Parties,
Having met in Milan from 1 to 12 December 2003 at the invitation of the Government of the
Italian Republic,
1. Expresses its profound gratitude to the Government of the Italian Republic for having
made it possible for the ninth session of the Conference of the Parties to be held in Milan;
2. Requests the Government of the Italian Republic to convey to the city and the people of
Milan the gratitude of the Conference of the Parties for the hospitality and warm welcome extended to
the participants.
