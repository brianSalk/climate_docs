Resolution 2/CP.4
Expression of gratitude to the Government of the Republic of Argentina
and to the City and people of Buenos Aires
The Conference of the Parties,
Having met in Buenos Aires from 2 to 14 November 1998 at the invitation of the
Government of the Republic of Argentina,
1. Expresses its profound gratitude to the Government of the Republic of Argentina
for having made it possible for the fourth session of the Conference of the Parties to be held in
Buenos Aires;
2. Requests the Government of the Republic of Argentina to convey to the City and
to the people of Buenos Aires the gratitude of the Conference of the Parties for the hospitality
and warm welcome extended to the participants.
8th plenary meeting
14 November 1998
